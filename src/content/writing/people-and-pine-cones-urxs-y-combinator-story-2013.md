[Detailed narrative of URX's founding and YC Summer 2013 experience. Starts with dropping a credit card in front of angel investor Bruno Bowden's apartment, leading to their first investment. During YC: Paul Graham's walks around Pioneer Way, PG saying "the opportunity is so big it's scary," partnering with Paul Buchheit (creator of Gmail). PG's pine cone metaphor -- "startups are like pine cones, all start small and look the same, but only a few realize their full potential." The name change from AdLast to URX after PG insisted.

Demo Day: chose to stay "off the record" per PG's recommendation. Raised from 31 investors in two and a half weeks after about forty meetings. Ends with reflection on the journey and gratitude.]

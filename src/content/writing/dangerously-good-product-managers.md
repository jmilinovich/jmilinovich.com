title: "Dangerously good product managers"
date: "2023-03-01"
---

[Comprehensive guide covering traits of exceptional PMs across six categories:]

**On Product Sense:** Are curious about technology, focus on problems not solutions, weigh all insights to make principled decisions, aim for simplicity.

**On Communication:** Know that less is more, write structured documents, maintain flexible schedules, avoid status meetings.

**On Planning:** Become domain experts, have strong opinions weakly held, clearly understand their goals, disagree early.

**On Project Management:** Don't drop the ball, drive for accountability, ensure clear next steps, identify and remove bottlenecks.

**On Relationships:** Understand the impact of their words, earn trust through excellence, proactively build rapport, present themselves authentically.

**On People Management:** Create psychological safety, follow Radical Candor, give feedback continuously, uplevel others with growth mindsets.

**On Managing Their Psychology:** Avoid hero mode, look at the horizon not their feet, get mad at the problem not the person, relentlessly disconnect.

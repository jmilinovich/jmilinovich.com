---
title: "Content alchemy"
date: "2020-05-02"
---

Content creators face one brutal constraint: time. Yet the pressure to publish keeps rising. More channels. More formats. More frequency. The math doesn't work.

Smart creators take their strongest content and transform it. That research piece you spent weeks perfecting? It's a slide deck, a video script, a podcast episode, a dozen Twitter threads. Each transformation reaches people who never saw the original.

Tools like Headliner and Designrr enable this. Start with tentpole content -- pieces worth transforming. Track transformation ratio instead of publish frequency.

This compounds over time. Each great piece becomes a foundation for ten more. Your back catalog becomes an asset, not just history.

The end goal isn't more content. It's more impact from your best work.

---
title: "Thoughts to docs"
date: "2024-09-25"
---

The best ideas emerge naturally -- during walks, showers, or while preparing coffee -- yet disappear when sitting down to write formally.

This workflow captures those fleeting thoughts and transforms them into finished documents within minutes rather than hours. The key: "Stop writing first drafts. Talk them."

**The System:** When inspiration strikes, record yourself thinking aloud on any device. Avoid structuring or editing during capture. Speak conversationally, as though explaining concepts to someone else. Submit the unpolished audio to an AI assistant for cleanup while preserving essential reasoning. After quick review and minor edits, you have a finished document -- a process taking 30 minutes instead of hours.

**Applications:** Technical documentation, design work, strategic planning, decision documents, technical specifications, creative briefs.

**Decision Document Example:** Traditional approach requires 2-3 hours. Voice-first requires 30 minutes.

Any voice recorder paired with an AI assistant produces results. The effectiveness derives from allowing thoughts to flow naturally, then leveraging AI to bridge raw thinking and polished outputs.

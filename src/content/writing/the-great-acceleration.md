This is the first essay in a series about living with AGI in society.

Time does not flow evenly through history. Some years contain more transformation than entire centuries. Some moments mark the boundary between what was and what will be.

We are living in such a moment -- and now we can see it unfolding in real time.

Earlier this week, a coalition of the world's most powerful technology companies announced a $500 billion investment in AI infrastructure -- the largest industrial mobilization since World War II. They called it The Stargate Project, and its goal was explicit: to build the foundation for artificial general intelligence.

[The essay discusses the acceleration of AI development, the predictability of scaling laws, timeline predictions for AGI surpassing college graduates by 2025 and human experts by 2027, the recursive self-improvement loop, and societal transformation. It concludes: "Welcome to the Great Acceleration. It's no longer coming -- it's here."]

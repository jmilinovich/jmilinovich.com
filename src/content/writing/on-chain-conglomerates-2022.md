---
title: "On Chain Conglomerates"
date: "2022-07-10"
---

Crypto Mullets represent an emerging class of substantial web3 enterprises, with on-chain conglomerates powered by revenue-generating NFTs serving as a primary distribution channel.

The Hub and Spoke design connects through ownership splits managed via NFTs. Hub mints ~1,000 NFTs with thirds allocated to third-party sales, operators, and treasury. Spokes are created for individual business entities.

Key advantages: standardizes protocols for launching blockchain subsidiaries, enables centralized investment while preserving autonomy, allows rolling investor participation. Challenges: lacks established off-chain to on-chain revenue conversion, requires careful NFT pricing, regulatory uncertainty.

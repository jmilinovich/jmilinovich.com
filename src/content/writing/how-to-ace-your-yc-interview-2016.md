---
title: "How to ace your YC interview"
date: "2016-04-15"
---

Six tips from URX's Summer 2013 batch experience:

1. **Quick, concise answers are key.** Interviews last ~10 minutes with rapid fire questions. Prepare ~50 expected questions with 2-3 bullet point responses. Use flashcard apps for self-testing.

2. **Know your 5 points.** Identify 3-5 most important things interviewers must hear. URX's points: long-standing team with industry experience, first version built, already have customers, know what we want from YC.

3. **Be ready to get grilled.** Our panel was Sam Altman, Geoff Ralston, and Garry Tan. Grilling started before we sat down. Each panel's style differs but substance is the same.

4. **Be a master of your own domain.** YC partners likely know as much about your domain as you do. Know the industry, not just your product.

5. **Make sure everyone speaks.** YC emphasizes founding team strength. Decide beforehand who answers which types of questions. Don't let one person run the show.

6. **Have a demo, but don't expect to show it.** Partners prefer hearing about the product over seeing it. Have one ready but don't rely on it.

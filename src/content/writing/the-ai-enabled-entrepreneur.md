I started my first company at 25 with three co-founders. My job was simple: talk to users, translate their needs into requirements, keep customers happy. The engineers built. The designer designed.

<!-- Image unavailable: original URL was https://images.spr.so/cdn-cgi/imagedelivery/j42No7y-dcokJuNgXeA0ig/b9fe972a-f928-4d21-a774-0cdb128596ea/Screenshot_2024-04-20_at_10.48.39_PM/w=3840,quality=90,fit=scale-down -->

Five years later, we started Aesthetic. Everyone had grown - designers could code, engineers could manage products, and I could finally build stuff myself.

<!-- Image unavailable: original URL was https://images.spr.so/cdn-cgi/imagedelivery/j42No7y-dcokJuNgXeA0ig/6bec2543-163c-4be8-9c37-fb418d7ab72a/Screenshot_2024-04-20_at_10.48.58_PM/w=3840,quality=90,fit=scale-down -->

Then GPT-4 dropped. I built a complete web app solo, then hired a freelancer for $10k to finish it. Pre-AI, this would've cost $50k minimum.

<!-- Image unavailable: original URL was https://images.spr.so/cdn-cgi/imagedelivery/j42No7y-dcokJuNgXeA0ig/c3431e54-7a09-48cf-b0b6-3b6164078c30/Screenshot_2024-04-20_at_11.14.23_PM/w=3840,quality=90,fit=scale-down -->

$10k vs $50k changes everything. Those small product ideas I used to ignore? Now they're viable $1k-$10k monthly revenue businesses. When you own 100%, that matters.

<!-- Image unavailable: original URL was https://images.spr.so/cdn-cgi/imagedelivery/j42No7y-dcokJuNgXeA0ig/c4a60845-1a2e-42c1-b3cc-c4136262c926/Screenshot_2024-04-20_at_11.58.03_PM/w=3840,quality=90,fit=scale-down -->

The numbers tell the story: 330k new businesses launch every quarter in the US. By 2030, it'll hit 1M. Why? The barriers are crumbling.

[Stanford HAI's AI Index, April 2024](https://hai.stanford.edu/news/ai-index-state-ai-13-charts?utm_source=www.theneurondaily.com&utm_medium=newsletter&utm_campaign=how-to-survive-the-ai-revolution)

AI isn't replacing jobs - it's creating entrepreneurs. While some roles will vanish, many more will transform. AI handles the parts, humans drive the vision.

<!-- Image unavailable: original URL was https://images.spr.so/cdn-cgi/imagedelivery/j42No7y-dcokJuNgXeA0ig/e3bcdbf2-2c79-4475-9642-5a97daf5ea34/Untitled/w=3840,quality=90,fit=scale-down -->

This shift isn't subtle. I'm 200x more capable as a developer than three years ago. Maybe I'm 2-3x better naturally, but AI tools gave me a 10-20x boost.

The question isn't whether AI will change entrepreneurship. The question is: What will you build with it?
